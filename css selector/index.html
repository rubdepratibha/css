PRESIDENT OF INDIA

Droupadi Murmu is the 15th and current President of India, holding office since July 2022. She made history as the first person from India's tribal communities, the youngest president, and the first president born in independent India.

Professional Life: She began working as a Junior Assistant in the Irrigation and Power Department of the Government of Odisha (1979–1983) before becoming an honorary teacher. She married Shyam Charan Murmu, a bank officer. The couple had three children, but she suffered tragic personal losses, including the deaths of her husband and two sons. She has one surviving daughter, Itishree Murmu.
